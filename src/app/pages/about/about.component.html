Biography

Ricardo Martínez is a music producer recognized for his innovative work in the industry. With years of experience, he has worked with numerous artists, combining classical and modern techniques to create unique sounds.

His private studio Rat Shack is set up with a collection of vintage and modern recording equipment and instruments, with a focus on comfort and quality sound.
