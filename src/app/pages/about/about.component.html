SOBRE EL PRODUCTOR

Ricardo Martínez es un productor musical reconocido por su trabajo innovador en la industria. Con años de experiencia, ha trabajado con numerosos artistas, combinando técnicas clásicas y modernas para crear sonidos únicos.

His private studio Rat Shack is setup with a collection of vintage and modern recording equipment and instruments, with a focus on comfort and quality sound. If you're interested in working with Ricardo, or have any questions, please visit the contact page and send him a message.
